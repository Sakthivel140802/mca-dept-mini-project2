About us

The department of Master of Computer Application offers a 2 years full-time Post Graduate degree program which is affiliated to Anna University and approved by AICTE New Delhi with an intake capacity of 60. At Excel Engineering College, semester pattern is followed for Master of Computer Application (MCA). An academic year consists of two semesters.

  • Database Management Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • System Software
  • Web Technologies
  • Networking

Our Vision

To be the source of bringing out globally competent pioneering computing Professionals, researchers, innovators, and entrepreneurs, thereby succeed and contribute value to the knowledge-based economy and society

Our Mission

  • To impart value-added technical education to the students and enrich their knowledge.
  • To develop innovative, challenging and quality software professionals.
  • To endeavor for continuous upgradation of technical expertise of students.
  • To mould the students into competent and creative technocrats to meet the growing global changes.

The Program

The curriculum of the program has been designed to cater to the ever changing needs and demands of IT industry. The focus is on holistic learning and empowering students to make significant contribution at all levels. The Master of computer application curriculum spreads over 6 semesters which includes courses for 5 semesters aimed at transmitting the broad spectrum of knowledge and 6th semester industrial training with intern ships in reputed organizations.
